Despre Homework Roots

Homework Roots is a web application for pupils, students, and others, which helps them organize their homework and projects and manage their time better. The platform includes an AI assistant that offers guidance, not solutions, to support learning as effectively as possible. In addition, the unique design and motivational quotes encourage students to achieve their goals.

About time management

Our goal is to help users complete their tasks faster and more efficiently by providing a calendar, AI, and many ways to motivate them to work.

About AI

We want to implement Artificial Intelligence in as many schools as possible.

Motivation

We offer motivation services such as a tree that grows with every task and motivational quotes.
